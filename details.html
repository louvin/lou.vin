Wedding Travel, Lodging and Schedule

June 27, 2020

Travel

The Philadelphia International Airport (PHL) is the closest nearby major airport (1-1.5 hours away from the venue by car). Rental cars are available from the airport. Click here for detailed driving directions.

Lodging

We've reserved a hotel block at the Holiday Inn Express & Suites Reading Airport (2389 Bernville Road, Reading, PA 19605). This is the closest hotel to the venue and it includes free breakfast.

To reserve a room in hotel block, call 1-800-HOLIDAY and give the code "FTW". Make sure you specify Reading, PA because there is also a Reading, CA!

Other lodging options include other hotels in the area or vacation rentals through sites like Airbnb or VRBO.

Venue

Bern Farm is located at 1160 Old Bernville Road, Reading, PA 19605. Click here for detailed driving directions. Parking is available on-site at the venue. The venue is fully wheelchair-accessible including both floors of the barn.
