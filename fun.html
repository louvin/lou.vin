Outdoors

Berks County Heritage Center

See where Devin worked during high school! Wide gravel trails along the Tulpehocken River connect to other county parks and historic sites. A museum with tours of some historic sites take place at the Heritage Center. Large open fields are great for frisbee golf, picnics or other activities. Learn more on the Heritage Center website.

Blue Marsh Lake

Blue Marsh is a large, man-made lake in Berks County with more than 30 miles of trails. Fun for hiking, picnics, and water sports. Learn more about Blue Marsh Lake here.

Cultural

GoggleWorks Center for the Arts

Once a goggle factory, the GoggleWorks is now a gallery and arts workshop open to the public. Admission is free. You'll see art exhibitions using diverse media, as well as in-progress art in the studios of the artists-in-residence. Check out the opening hours and galleries on the GoggleWorks website.
